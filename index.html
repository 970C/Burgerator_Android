Burgerator for Android

Team Hamburgerler's CS480/481 project to port the Burgerator iOS app to android + a 2.0 revision

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Welcome to The Hamburgerlers' CS 480/481 senior project home page.

Our CS480 project is a port and update of the Burgerator iOS app to Android. We are working with the app's founder, Ammar Shallal from NYC. The update will include the ability to browse the app without the need to log in, capturing location data upon opening the app, and possibly updates to the social functionality - allowing users to more easily follow other Burgerator users.

The Hamburgerlers' vision is to fulfill the expectations of our client by porting the successful IOS Burgerator app to Adriod. In this process we will perform the desired upgrades to the app to take it to the next level, Burgerator 2.0!
